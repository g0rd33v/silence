Do nothing. Just be.

Silence measures how much of your day still belongs to you. To count only real silence — not performed silence — it needs two signals from this device.

Microphone

We read a single number — the ambient volume. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored. Ever.

Motion

We check that the phone is resting still. If you pick it up, the session pauses until you put it back down.

Download speech model?

A ~500 MB on-device speech model will be downloaded so Silence can transcribe your ideas during Infinity sessions. Stays on your device — no audio ever leaves your phone. One-time download. Use Wi-Fi.

Catch your ideas?

During this Infinity session, Silence will catch any stray ideas you say out loud and attach them to the log. Transcription happens on your device — no audio leaves your phone.

First time: a ~500 MB speech model will download to your device. One-time — cached forever after that. Use Wi-Fi.
